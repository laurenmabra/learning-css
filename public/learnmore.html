Benefits of Knowing Photography in Modern Marketing

Home | | Learn More

  1. Gains attention

    2. 1.5 billion units of content is being shared online. Now more than ever, marketers need to understand how to make their clients stand out. Pictures increase people's attention and willingness to read by 80%

  2. Increases chances of going viral

    3. We see pictures and videos go viral all the time. Visual content goes viral when users like it enough to share among their own network which means massive amounts of exposure for the original publisher/creator.

  3. Improves understanding

    4. Some of the strongest and most powerful brands have gotten to where they are using photographs to potray their message and connect with their audience.

  4. Influences emotions

    5. Often times, consumers are influenced by their emotions when making purchasing decisions. Photographs help transmit the company's message more clearly and can elicit powerful emotions from people.

  5. Helps drive sales

    6. High quality images not only help with branding but also show the consumers how the product or service being offered could improve their life.
